Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Within the fields of information technology and businesses, several tools have risen in popularity based on their utilization and benefits brought to users. Notably, the technology of generative artificial intelligence (AI) is a prominent tool that has been widely implemented throughout the business environment, displaying a rise in significance. Generative AI is a tool in which content is generated from user input and its output ranges from video, audio, and text based responses.
